Our numbers (.pdf) show you are overpaying for data storage by 10-100x. When you pay for data storage you also are paying for employees, servers, and profit margins. We eliminate those costs by automating the entire network.

Trustless & Secure, by Design

When you have central sources of data, security breaches occur [1] [2]. Because our network is decentralized, we don't have this problem. There is no database to breach, no cache of data to hack. Don't trust us, you don't have to.
